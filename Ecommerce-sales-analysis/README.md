# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales Explorer

Welcome to the **E-Commerce Sales Explorer**!
This project dives into a retail sales dataset to uncover trends about **revenue, profits, regions, and products**.

Think of it as a **data detective adventure** 🔍 where we explore how an online store performs and what drives its sales.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze e-commerce sales data and answer important business questions such as:

📦 Which product categories generate the most sales?
🌍 Which regions bring the highest revenue?
📈 How do sales change over time?
💰 Which products are the most profitable?

---

# 📊 Questions We Explore

### 1️⃣ Which category sells the most?

We compare sales across categories like:

* Technology
* Furniture
* Office Supplies

📌 Insight: Identify which category drives the most revenue.

---

### 2️⃣ Which region generates the most sales?

We analyze performance across regions such as:

* East
* West
* Central
* South

📌 Insight: Discover where the business performs best geographically.

---

### 3️⃣ How do sales change over time?

Using order dates, we explore **monthly sales trends**.

📌 Insight: Detect growth patterns and seasonal trends.

---

### 4️⃣ Which products sell the most?

We identify the **Top 10 best-selling products**.

📌 Insight: Helps businesses understand which products customers love.
