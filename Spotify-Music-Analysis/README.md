# 🎧 Spotify Music Data Explorer

Welcome to the **Spotify Music Data Explorer**!
This project dives into a Spotify tracks dataset to uncover patterns about **artists, genres, song popularity, and audio features**.

Think of it as a **music discovery adventure powered by data** 🎶📊

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze Spotify track data and explore questions like:

🎤 Which artists appear the most in the dataset?
🎶 What genres dominate Spotify playlists?
🔥 What makes a song popular?
⏱ How long are most songs?

By analyzing these patterns, we can better understand **music trends and listener preferences**.

---

# 🔍 Questions We Explore

## 🎤 Who are the most frequent artists?

We analyze which artists appear most often in the dataset.

📌 Insight: Some artists dominate music streaming platforms with a large number of tracks.

---

## 🎶 Which genres are most popular?

We explore the distribution of music genres.

📌 Insight: Certain genres appear significantly more often, revealing trends in listener preferences.

---

## 📈 How popular are songs on Spotify?

We examine the **distribution of song popularity scores**.

📌 Insight: Most songs fall within moderate popularity ranges, while a few become massive hits.

---

## ⏱ How long are songs?

Using track duration data, we analyze the **average song length**.

📌 Insight: Most songs fall within the typical **2–4 minute range**.

---

## 🔥 What audio features influence music?

We explore correlations between features like:

* Danceability
* Energy
* Valence
* Popularity

📌 Insight: Some song characteristics may influence how engaging or popular a track becomes.
